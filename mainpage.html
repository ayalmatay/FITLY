Services

Gym


In our gymnasiums there are gyms that allow to work out all groups of muscles. We will help you achieve your goals, whether it’s muscle mass, weight loss or preparation for competition.

Personal training


Personal training is the ideal solution for those who value their time and want a guaranteed result. You can study in a separate gymnasium, you can choose from any of the group programs.

Boxing


Boxing develops endurance and skeletal muscle system. Training is designed for both men and women and takes place in our hall five times a week.

Group training


Group training works on strengthening and healing of the body: the work of internal organs is improved, the stability of the nervous system and the flexibility of the body is increased.
